She had to land partway through her flight in Northern Ireland when she had problems with her plane 15 hours into the flight. In 1940, people found a female skeleton on the island of Nikumaroro in the South Pacific that people now believe is likely Earhart. New forensic analysis suggests that she may have crash-landed and died.
